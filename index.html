A

Growth & Revenue Ops

The goal: stop losing revenue to slow or forgotten follow-up. Early-stage deals rarely die because the product is wrong — they die because a reply sat unanswered for six hours, a promising call never got logged, or you ran out of hours before reaching the next prospect. These systems take the human bottleneck out of your revenue motion: every inbound signal — a reply, a finished call, a fresh lead — triggers the right next action in seconds, whether or not anyone's at their desk. You stay human where it counts (the actual conversation) and hand the chasing, sorting, and logging to a system that never gets busy, tired, or distracted.

SYS·01

Founder-Led Pipeline

Reply lands, gets classified, CRM updates, you get pinged — in under two minutes. Respond while they're still thinking about you.

  • Connects your inbox, enrichment source, and CRM
  • Classifies every reply: interested, not now, unsubscribe, spam
  • Routes hot leads to HubSpot or Pipedrive, context pre-filled
  • Telegram or Slack alert the moment someone's ready
OutcomeFirst response under 2 min. Zero manual handoffs.
SYS·02

Meeting-to-CRM Pipe

Every sales call logged, summarized, and actioned automatically — so no deal stalls because you forgot to write it up.

  • Connects your meeting recorder to your CRM
  • Extracts action items, objections, and next steps
  • Updates the deal record without you touching it
  • Drafts the follow-up email for your approval
OutcomeNo deal stalls because a call went unlogged.
SYS·03

Cold Outreach Engine

Targeted outreach to your exact ICP, personalized at scale — and nothing sends until you approve it.

  • Scrapes and verifies leads matching your buyer profile
  • Writes a personalized opener for every contact
  • Pushes to your sending tool automatically
  • You review and approve before anything goes out
OutcomeWake up to replies from people who actually fit.
B

Reliability, Observability & Security

SRE·01

Observability-in-a-Box

Know something's wrong before your customers tell you. Dashboards, SLOs, and alerts that actually mean something.

  • Instruments your services and ships clean dashboards
  • Defines SLOs/SLIs tied to what users feel, not vanity metrics
  • Tunes alerts to kill noise — page only on what matters
  • Unifies logs, metrics, and traces into one pane
OutcomeSee incidents forming. Stop hearing it from customers first.
SRE·02

Incident & Reliability Automation

When something breaks at 2am, the system runs the first ten minutes for you — so a human doesn't start from zero.

  • Auto-opens the incident channel and pages the right people
  • Runs diagnostic runbooks and posts findings automatically
  • Drafts the postmortem straight from the timeline
  • Tracks error budgets so reliability work gets prioritized
OutcomeLower mean-time-to-resolution. Less on-call burnout.
SRE·03

Security & Compliance Automation

Turn scanner noise and audit busywork into a system that handles itself — and surfaces only what's actually dangerous.

  • Triages findings (Wiz, Qualys, Snyk) and routes real risk only
  • Collects SOC 2 / ISO evidence automatically on a schedule
  • Flags exposed secrets and stale access for review
  • Enriches alerts with context before a human ever sees them
OutcomeAudits stop being fire drills. Real risk surfaces first.
